Privacy Policy

Plain-English version: we don't sell your data, we encrypt the sensitive bits, and you can delete everything any time. The longer version is below.

Last updated 1 May 2026.

1. Who we are

This policy describes how Pregnancy AI Limited ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses and protects information when you use our app, this website, or contact us. If you have any questions about this policy, write to privacy@pregnancyai.app.

2. What we collect

The information we hold falls into a few buckets:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and a hashed password.
  • Pregnancy information — anything you enter into the app, including due dates, weeks, kick logs, appointments, and birth-plan answers.
  • Photos and uploads — ultrasound scans, pregnancy tests, medical documents, baby-face source photos, and any other images you upload to a tool.
  • Conversations with Lola — the messages you send, and the responses you receive.
  • Device and usage data — basic information about the device you use, the version of the app, and the screens you look at, used to fix bugs and improve the experience.

3. How we use it

We use your information to operate the service, personalise Lola to your week and history, send you relevant notifications, fix problems, and keep the app secure.

We do not sell your personal information. We don't use your conversations or uploads to train AI models without your explicit, opt-in consent.

4. Who we share it with

We share information only with vendors that help us run the service — for example, our cloud hosting provider, our customer support tool, and our payments processor. These vendors are contractually limited to using your data only for the purposes we tell them to.

We will disclose information when legally required to do so, and to protect the safety of users.

5. Where we store data

Your data is stored on encrypted servers in the European Union and the United Kingdom. Sensitive uploads — scans, tests, medical documents — are encrypted at rest and in transit.

6. How long we keep it

We keep your account information for as long as you have an account. Photos and uploads used in single-shot tools (like the baby-face generator) are deleted after processing. Conversations and pregnancy information are kept while your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete everything within 30 days.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access your data, correct it, delete it, restrict our processing, and object to processing. The simplest way to do most of this is from inside the app — go to Settings → Data. For anything else, email us.

8. Cookies

This website uses a small number of cookies — strictly necessary ones to make the site work, and optional analytics cookies to understand how people use it. We ask before setting anything optional.

9. Children

Pregnancy AI is intended for adults aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 16.

10. Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top and let you know in the app or by email when the change is significant.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, email privacy@pregnancyai.app. You can also lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

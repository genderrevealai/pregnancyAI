Getting started

Pregnancy AI is a companion app for the forty weeks of pregnancy. You can chat with Lola, our AI midwife, predict your baby's gender from a scan, check pregnancy test lines, and use a dozen tools that handle everything from naming the baby to packing the hospital bag.
Download Pregnancy AI free from the App Store or Google Play. The basic features work without a subscription; the full toolkit and unlimited Lola chats are unlocked with a small monthly plan that includes a 7-day free trial.

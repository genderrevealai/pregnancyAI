Frequently asked

The things people ask before they download.

Pregnancy AI is a companion app for the forty weeks of pregnancy. You can chat with Lola, our AI midwife, upload your scan for a gender prediction, check pregnancy test lines, and use a dozen tools that handle everything from birth plans to hospital bag checklists. It's designed to sit quietly beside your pregnancy — not replace your midwife or doctor.
Lola is an AI model trained on guidance from established clinical sources like NHS and NICE. She's brilliant for general questions, reassurance and triaging your worries — but she's not a substitute for your midwife or GP. If something feels off, please call them.
Your scans, photos and chats are encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell personal data, and you can permanently delete your account and everything in it from inside the app at any time.
Lola is trained to recognise red-flag symptoms and will always tell you to contact your maternity unit or emergency services if something sounds urgent. The app isn't an emergency service — if you think something is wrong, please call your provider directly.
Predictions improve dramatically the further along you are. From around 12 weeks the model is highly accurate on a clear scan; earlier than that it's a fun guess with a confidence score. We always show you how confident we are, and we never claim to replace a sonographer.
Pregnancy AI is free to download with a generous free tier. A small monthly subscription unlocks unlimited Lola chats, scan predictions, and the full toolkit. There's a 7-day free trial.

Tool · Test Line Checker

Is that a line, or am I imagining it?

Take a photo of your pregnancy test and our line checker enhances the contrast so a real line is unmistakable. Track tests across days to watch the line darken.

Try in the app Back to all tools
[ Line checker preview ]

How it works.

  • Snap your test. Take a photo within the test's recommended read window.
  • Let us enhance it. We boost contrast on the test strip so a faint line is visible.
  • Track over days. Save tests and compare them side-by-side as the line darkens.
  • Get a kind second opinion. Optionally share to our community for an extra set of eyes.
Important

This isn't medical advice.

An enhanced photo is still just a photo. A faint line, a clear line or no line at all should be confirmed with another test or with your GP — especially if you've been trying for a while.

Stop squinting.
